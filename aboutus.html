ABOUT US

Le Complex is a team of first-year engineering students from NMAMIT college focused on creating innovative solutions to everyday challenges. Our financial tool is designed to help people manage their finances more effectively, offering features that simplify budgeting, savings, and financial planning. We're driven by a passion for technology and a commitment to making personal finance accessible to everyone.

KEY FEATURES:
1.Budget Manager which helps allocate finances between savings and expenses
2.Investment planner which offers investment plans based on income,financial goal and risk apetite
3.Savings Tracker which tracks current savings and provides future growth projections
4.Stock recommedition which suggestts stocks tailored to low,moderate or high-risk levels
5.Risk Assessment Calculator calculates a personal risk score to aid investment decisions
6.Financial Health Check analyzes and provides insights into overall financial well being
